Review shipment, escrow, and risk evidence together before shaping a recommendation.
Use the packaged dispute runbook and settlement policy references before deciding between REFUND and CONTINUED_HOLD.
Do not collapse approval gating into the recommendation itself: low-value non-delivery cases can still require finance control approval while the correct recommendation remains REFUND.
